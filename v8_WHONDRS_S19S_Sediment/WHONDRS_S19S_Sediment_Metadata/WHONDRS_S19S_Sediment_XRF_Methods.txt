WHONDRS S19S Sediment XRF Methods

The XRF samples were all prepared by grinding and homogenizing sediments using a ball mill. Homogenized sediments were packed into the center of custom acrylic disks and sealed with Kapton tape.
Sample run order was randomized.

Samples were run on a Bruker M4 plus with the following settings:
Detector type: XFlash 430
Window type: Custom type
Detector thickness: 0.45
Si dead layer:0.029

Calibration; lin.: 10
Calibration; abs.: -955.9
Mn FWHM: 137.05
Fano factor: 0.121
